ABOUT US

Welcome to QuadrantDance dance studio.

We are a studio founded by dance artists from all over the world, dedicated to providing high-quality, diverse dance education and performance opportunities for the community.

At QuadrantDance, we encourage students to explore different dance styles and techniques to improve their body awareness and dance expression. Our coaching team has rich teaching and performance experience and can provide professional, personalized guidance and support to students.

SERVICES

Our courses include various dance styles such as jazz, modern dance, ballet, street dance, Latin dance, and belly dance, suitable for students of different ages and levels.

In addition to dance courses, we regularly hold performances and workshops, allowing students to showcase their talents live while interacting and learning from other dancers.

We also collaborate with local communities to provide free dance classes for children and teenagers, enabling more people to have access to and enjoy the beauty of dance.
